Who We Are

Not your average freelancers.

PixelCrew is a five-person creative digital studio run by undergraduates who refused to wait until graduation to start building real things. We met in university, discovered we had complementary skills, and decided to combine them into something bigger than any of us could do alone.

We take on frontend development, graphic design, AI-assisted web builds, Python scripting, and brand identity work — all part-time, all remote, all delivered with the kind of hunger you only get from people who are still proving themselves.

We work on monthly retainers and hourly contracts. No hidden fees. No nonsense. Just work.
